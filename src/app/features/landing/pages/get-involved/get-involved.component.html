Be a Voice That Moves Others

It starts with one voice. One message. One moment that joins a growing chorus, sharing hope, connection, love, and understanding with every person touched by cancer.

Being a WondrVoices Ambassador means letting your voice be that spark, the one that ripples outward, taking its own shape in your community. Where you share it, how you express it, and who it touches will be uniquely yours.

What Ambassadors Do

When you become a WondrVoices Ambassador, you join a movement built on connection. Your role is simple: take one small action to spread kindness, and watch it grow.

You might:

  • Invite your local spots: cafés, libraries, galleries, to become WondrSpots
  • Host a card-making table, at a farmers market, art fair, or park bench
  • Write and decorate a card yourself, then encourage friends to do the same
  • Spread awareness on Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, or Nextdoor

Make Kindness Go Viral

Kindness has momentum. Researchers have found that a single good deed can ripple outward, touching people up to three degrees removed from the original act.

That’s why your voice matters.
Each card, each conversation, each new WondrSpot you inspire becomes part of a growing chorus, carrying hope, connection, love, and understanding into communities you may never see.

You don’t have to walk in someone’s shoes to care.
You just have to be willing to reach.

Be the drop.
The quiet spark that sets kindness in motion, until it ripples farther than you’ll ever know.
