Be a Voice That Moves Others

WondrVoices Ambassadors are everyday changemakers who help kindness go further, online and in real life. Whether you’re sharing on social, hosting an event, or inspiring local spaces to join, your voice helps spread hope.

What Ambassadors do

As an ambassador, you’ll help build a ripple effect of kindness and connection. You don’t need special skills — just a desire to make a difference in your community and online.

  • Encourage local spots (like cafés, libraries, galleries) to become WondrSpots
  • Host a meetup or popup card-making event
  • Share stories and videos that inspire others to join
  • Help distribute cards to nearby cancer centers or hospitals
  • Spread awareness on Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, or Nextdoor

Make Kindness Go Viral

Every month, we highlight ambassadors who make the biggest splash, online or offline.
From viral reels to standout events, your creativity could be featured here.
