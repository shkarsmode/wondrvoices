Bring WondrVoices to Your Community

WondrSpots invite visitors to pause, create, and give back. Whether you're a café, gallery, bookstore, or studio, becoming a WondrSpot adds another purpose to your place.

Become a WondrSpot

WondrSpots are spaces such as libraries, clinics, coffee shops, classrooms, community centers, or kitchen tables where kindness takes shape. They are places where anyone can pause, reflect, and create something meaningful for someone facing cancer or serious illness.

By becoming a WondrSpot, you’re helping turn your community into a wellspring of compassion, creativity, and connection. Whether you host a one-time letter-writing event or set up a permanent card-making station, you're offering something rare and needed: a moment of care from one human to another.

What You’re Creating

When someone sits down at a WondrSpot, they’re reminded:

  • You are not alone.
  • Your words matter.
  • Even small acts can ripple outward.

Each card or artwork created will be anonymously delivered to someone navigating a serious diagnosis. These messages often go to hospitals, cancer centers, or hospice programs, offering comfort in a moment of uncertainty.
