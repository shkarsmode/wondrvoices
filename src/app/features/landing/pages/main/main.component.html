Share hope, one message at a time

Every card, note, and voice shared here is a small act of kindness that can brighten even the hardest days. Whether it's a child's drawing, a handwritten message, or an encouraging voice, each one reminds patients they are not alone in their fight. Join a growing community of people spreading encouragement to those who need it most.

Submit a Card

Share your creativity to lift someone’s spirit. Whether it’s a drawing, handwritten note, or warm words — your work will be printed and delivered to a patient who needs a moment of comfort and connection.

Become a WondrSpot

Start a card-making project in your school, workplace, or community. WondrSpots help teach, heal, build teams, and help spread kindness through handmade messages of hope.

Help Us Grow

Support WondrVoices by sharing our mission or donating. Your help ensures a new wave of care can rise, reach more patients, and grow a movement of care.

Why We Started WondrVoices

What began as a simple way to comfort patients with handmade cards has grown into a small but meaningful movement. WondrVoices helps foster connection and kindness through messages that remind patients they are not alone.
